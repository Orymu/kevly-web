# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-15

Kevly values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices you have when you use Kevly.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### Account Information

- Your email address
- Your password if you create an account with email and password
- Basic profile information, such as your name and profile photo
- Information related to email verification, password reset, and account security

### Product and Activity Information

- Spaces you create or join
- Members, guests, and invitations connected to those spaces
- Expense, settlement, balance, and summary information
- Receipt information you upload, scan, review, or edit

### Images and Files

- Profile photos you upload
- Receipt images you capture or upload
- Information extracted from receipts to help you review and organize expenses

### Device and App Information

- Session and sign-in information
- Notification token information if you enable notifications
- Basic device or platform information needed to operate the app
- Logs and diagnostic information used to keep the service secure and reliable

### Information Stored on Your Device

Kevly may store limited information on your device to support the app experience, such as:

- sign-in state
- cached profile images
- temporary receipt scan or review data
- app preferences

## 2. Permissions We May Request

Kevly may request access to certain device features when needed for a product feature.

### Camera

Kevly may request camera access so you can:

- take a profile photo
- scan or capture a receipt

### Photo Library

Kevly may request access to your photo library so you can select an existing image, such as a profile photo.

### Notifications

Kevly may request permission to send notifications if notifications are available for your account or device.

You can decline these permissions, but some features may not work properly without them.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- create and manage your account
- sign you in and keep your account secure
- provide the core Kevly product experience
- let you create spaces, invite others, and manage shared expenses
- process receipts and show editable receipt details
- display and manage profile images
- send service-related emails, such as account verification, password reset, and account deletion notices
- send notifications if you enable them
- prevent abuse, fraud, and security problems
- improve and maintain Kevly

## 4. Receipt and Image Handling

If you use receipt or image-related features, Kevly may process those files so the feature works as intended.

For example:

- receipt images may be used to help extract and organize expense details
- profile photos may be used to display your account identity in the app

In some cases, Kevly may use third-party service providers to support file storage, processing, or receipt extraction.

## 5. When We Share Information

We may share information in the following situations:

### With Other Users

If you use shared spaces or invite other people, some of your information may be visible to those users, such as:

- your name
- your profile photo
- shared expense and receipt-related records

### With Service Providers

We may share information with companies that help us operate Kevly, such as providers that support:

- hosting and storage
- notifications
- email delivery
- file processing
- security, monitoring, and technical operations

These providers are only permitted to use information as needed to provide services to Kevly.

### For Legal or Safety Reasons

We may disclose information if reasonably necessary to:

- comply with applicable law or legal process
- protect Kevly, our users, or the public
- enforce our terms or investigate misuse
- support a business transaction such as a merger, acquisition, or financing

## 6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to:

- provide the service
- maintain account and product functionality
- comply with legal obligations
- resolve disputes
- protect the security and integrity of Kevly

Some information may be retained longer when needed for recordkeeping, fraud prevention, security, or compliance.

## 7. Account Deletion

You may be able to request account deletion.

Based on Kevly’s current account deletion flow:

- deletion is scheduled with a grace period before finalization
- you may be able to cancel the request during that period
- after finalization, your account information is removed or de-identified as appropriate

Some historical records may be retained in a form that no longer directly identifies you where retention is necessary for legal, security, accounting, fraud prevention, or record-integrity purposes.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Children

Kevly is not intended for children who are not legally permitted to use the service without appropriate consent. If you believe a child has provided personal information improperly, contact us so we can review the issue.

## 10. International Processing

Your information may be processed or stored in countries other than your own, depending on where Kevly and its service providers operate.

## 11. Your Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may be able to:

- access or update certain account information
- remove your profile photo
- manage device permissions such as camera, photo library, and notifications
- request account deletion
- choose not to use features that require certain permissions

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may provide notice through the app, website, or other appropriate means. The updated version will include a revised “Last updated” date.

If you have privacy questions or requests, you can contact us at:
